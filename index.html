The Legend of Veer Da Chappar

We began with a simple mission: to serve honest, flavorful, and incredibly satisfying homestyle Punjabi and North Indian comfort food that reminds you of home. Nestled right near the Sikanderpur Metro pillar, we've grown into a beloved destination for everyone from corporate workers heading home to late-night party-goers craving a heavy meal at 3:00 AM.

Our food is prepared using traditional recipes, high-quality spices, and that essential generous dollop of butter. From our famous giant Parathas to our rich gravies, every dish is a celebration of rustic North Indian cuisine.
